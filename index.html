My Story

My name is Juliana Mota, I'm a Multimedia Designer student at Copenhagen School of Design and Technology (KEA) and I also have a master degree in Architecture from Lisbon University Institute.

After I was done with my studies in Portugal, I applied for an internship in architecture offices around Copenhagen. I got accepted and I moved to Denmark in 2013 to do a 1-year internship at Primus Arkitekter and a 1-year internship at Core Arkitecter. During these 2 years, I mastered Adobe programs, improved in 3D SketchUp, gained more knowledge in the architecture field, and built professional competences.

In 2017, I decided to go back to school and learn more about design and coding. I keep on working with Adobe Indesign, Photoshop, Illustrator and Premiere. I am also learning building responsible website using tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JSON.
